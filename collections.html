Collections



Books

More than 200,000 volumes of print materials, including 600+ periodicals.

Journals

Search magazines, journals, and newspapers in print and digital.

Film & Video

Over 6,000 unique titles in a variety of formats.

E-Resources

Databases for articles, eBooks, images, statistics and more. Accessible on campus and at home.

Research Guides

Subject specific guides to help you with your research.

Image Collections

The Pratt Institute Libraries make available a wide range of image resources to support teaching and learning.

Equipment

Computers, Projectors, and AV Equipment.

Artists’ Books

The Artists' Book collection encompasses books crafted, published, produced or altered by an artist.

Zines

The Libraries’ Zine Collection focuses on locally-produced titles and are selected for artistic as well as literary content.

Theses

The Brooklyn Campus Library acts as a repository for Pratt's graduate theses.

Special Collections

Rare and limited editions of seminal works in the fields of art, architecture and design.

Archives

Historical papers, images, and artifacts that document the history of Pratt Institute.
