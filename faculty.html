Faculty Support



Library Liaisons

Librarians are assigned to subjects based on their backgrounds and expertise. In addition to selecting materials for each department's needs, they work closely with faculty, staff, and students. Your librarian would love to hear from you and learn more about your courses and research interests.

Instruction Request

Librarians can help your students learn the best ways to find and evaluate information by providing custom-designed library instruction sessions tailored to your course.

Course Reserves

Books can be placed on reserve by faculty so students have access to them all semester. Faculty can submit items for reserve by filling out the form below. Read Faculty Reserve Guidelines.


Film / Video Reserves

Faculty may place any films from the collection on one-time or full semester reserve for their classes by filling out the form below.

Room Reservations

The Libraries oversee two rooms for classes, presentations, and meetings. These rooms are equipped for a variety of audiovisual needs.

ConnectNY

Request books outside of the Pratt Collections with a quick delivery and a loan period of 42 days.

Interlibrary Loan

Interlibrary Loan (ILL) assists scholarly research by obtaining library materials not owned by the Pratt Libraries.

Intercampus Loan

Can't make it to our other library location? Request a book or DVD to be shipped between the Brooklyn or Manhattan Campus Library.

Suggest a Purchase

The Pratt Institute Libraries welcomes suggestions for material purchases which support the teaching, study, and research at the Institute. New materials for Reserve can be purchased through this form as well.
